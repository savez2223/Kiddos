Teacher Training Programme

Empowering educators with world-class training for early childhood education.

📚
🌟

The Preschool Teacher Training Program is an initiative of GOOGLAi SCHOOL Learning. As a pioneer in ECCE (Early Childhood Care and Education), we have set unmatched standards in the CDE (Child Development and Education) space.

With the introduction of NEP regulating preschool education in India, it is mandatory that all preschool teachers are trained in early childhood education to be able to teach at the foundational stage.

Child Introduction

Nurturing young minds for a lifetime of learning and growth.

🌟
🎈

Children receive their most important education even before the age of six. Until this age, each morning for children is the dawn of another wonderful adventure. Our focus is not only on developing academic skills, but also intellectual, emotional, linguistic, physical, social, and moral skills, which will ensure the all-round development of children.

We believe that children are active learners who learn best by interacting with nature, other children, and adults in child-centered activities. We provide opportunities for the child to grow and develop while reading, listening, and playing.

Love and affection are the hallmarks of all these activities. Child-friendly equipment, engaging toys, and games help the child to listen and discover, imagine and create.
