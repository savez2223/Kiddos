GOOGLAi SCHOOL is an innovative and technologically advanced educational institution that started its journey in 2024. Our aim is to provide children with a safe, secure, and interactive learning environment.

Our school's curriculum is specially designed to take into account each child's individual learning needs. At GOOGLAi SCHOOL, we use advanced intelligent systems that make each student's learning experience personalized and efficient.

By using AI and advanced technologies in our school, our AI-powered approach provides children with a dynamic and adaptive learning environment where they can grow at their own learning pace.

F A Q f o r K i d s

We understand your concern about handing over your child to the right hands. Here are answers to common questions parents ask before choosing GOOGLAi SCHOOL for their little explorers!

🌟 What is the minimum age limit for admission?

The minimum age is 2½ years on April 1 of the relevant year.

🎨 How many programs are there for my child?

We offer Play Group, Nursery, LKG, UKG, and School Activities, designed for different ages and learning abilities.

👩‍🏫 What is the teacher-student ratio?

Our ratio is 1:1:20 (1 class teacher, 1 activity teacher, 1 caretaker per 20 students).

⏰ What are the school hours?

School hours average between 4 to 5 hours per day.

💻 Do you have digital learning?

Yes! We use AI tools for a fun, future-ready digital learning experience.

🌍 What’s your learning philosophy?

Excellence in Education, Guiding Opportunities, Outstanding Goals, Learning Global, Advancing Intelligence!

🧼 Are classrooms sanitized?

Yes, we have special staff who clean and sanitize everything daily!

👪 How much parental involvement is needed?

We love 100% involvement! Join our seminars and workshops for your child’s best progress.

⚽ How much playtime do kids get?

At least 30 minutes daily for indoor and outdoor fun!

🏫 Can I tour the school first?

Yes, come visit us! Contact GOOGLAi SCHOOL to schedule a tour.
