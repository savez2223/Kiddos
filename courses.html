Our Program

Play school programs help children develop social, language, and motor skills. They also help children learn how to share, take turns, and follow directions.

Pre-Nursery

Duration: 4-4½ hours per day

Limited Seat: 20

Ratio: (One class teacher: One activity teacher: One care taker)

Helps children develop basic mannerisms, social skills, and language.

Nursery

Duration: 4-4½ hours per day

Limited Seat: 20

Ratio: (One class teacher: One activity teacher: One care taker)

Builds on foundational skills and introduces structured learning, including early literacy and numeracy.

LKG

Duration: 4-4½ hours per day

Limited Seat: 20

Ratio: (One class teacher: One activity teacher: One care taker)

Prepares children for formal schooling with a focus on language and cognitive skills.

UKG

Duration: 4-4½ hours per day

Limited Seat: 20

Ratio: (One class teacher: One activity teacher: One care taker)

Refines children's academic, social, and creative abilities.
